# Determines whether the recruit was an in-state recruit, from a bordering state, or from out of the region for the college

Determines whether the recruit was an in-state recruit, from a bordering
state, or from out of the region for the college

## Usage

``` r
bordering_states(recruits_df)
```

## Arguments

- recruits_df:

  Recruiting dataframe (*DF*, required)
